Priority must therefore favor:

1. Governance consistency
2. Website and information architecture
3. Canonical educational content
4. Trust infrastructure
5. Sustainable content operations

**Brain Health**  
→ **Education**

→ **Products**

## 32. Final Priority Principles

The purpose of priorities is to reduce distraction.

Not every useful idea is a current priority.

Not every urgent-looking task is strategically urgent.

The website foundation comes before content scale.

Canonical content comes before derivative volume.

Trust infrastructure comes before product promotion.

Evidence comes before formulation.

Formula comes before marketing.

Audit comes before implementation.

A clear human decision comes before irreversible work.

NervLock should not attempt to do everything at once.

It should complete the highest-leverage foundation, verify that it works, and then expand deliberately.
