Core Ingredient 01
Phosphatidylserine (PS)
300mg · Neuronal Membrane Phospholipid
🧬
🧬 Notified functional ingredient (MFDS-approved)

A key phospholipid in neuronal cell membranes, phosphatidylserine is involved in cell-to-cell signaling and brain energy metabolism. The Korean MFDS (Ministry of Food and Drug Safety) recognizes PS as a functional ingredient that may help improve memory, backed by an extensive body of clinical research.

1
MFDS-recognized function: may help improve memory

Core Ingredient 02
rTG Omega-3
Re-esterified Triglyceride Form · EPA+DHA 900–1,000mg
🐟
🐟 rTG form — superior absorption vs. ethyl ester

DHA — the primary structural fatty acid of brain grey matter (~15%) — and EPA for circulatory support, delivered in re-esterified triglyceride (rTG) form for optimal absorption. The MFDS recognizes EPA+DHA as a functional ingredient that may help improve memory and support healthy blood flow.

MFDS-recognized: may help improve memory + support blood flow

rTG form delivers superior bioavailability vs. standard ethyl ester
