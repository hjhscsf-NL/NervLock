Do You Experience This?

Why Neural Health
Demands Consistent Care

After age 40, the body's ability to support neural tissue declines, while chronic low-grade inflammation accumulates — making proactive neural health management increasingly important.

🖐️

Tingling & Numbness

A persistent tingling or prickling sensation in the hands and feet, especially after prolonged sitting or upon waking. May signal a need for neural health attention.

🔥

Burning Sensation

A burning or electric-shock-like feeling in the extremities. Commonly experienced by individuals with heightened neural sensitivity.

🌙

Nocturnal Discomfort

Neural discomfort that intensifies at night, disrupting restorative sleep. The impact on quality of life is significant and warrants proactive management.

⚡

Radiating Sensations

Electric-like sensations radiating from the neck or lower back into the limbs — a sign that neural pathways may benefit from targeted nutritional support.

⏳

Persistent Discomfort

Long-lasting neural discomfort that does not resolve on its own. Sustained nutritional management of neural health is often the most effective approach.

😔

Impact on Daily Life

Chronic neural discomfort affects energy, mood, and overall quality of life. Addressing the underlying nutritional environment is key to daily wellbeing.

Why these two core ingredients? — Dr.H, Neurologist

In neural health nutrition, what matters most is not the number of ingredients, but the depth of evidence. Curcumin and Aflapin® support the neural inflammatory environment through complementary mechanisms — NF-κB and 5-LOX pathways respectively — with synergistic effects that exceed either ingredient alone. Rather than including many marginally effective ingredients, I chose to include therapeutically meaningful doses of the two most rigorously studied compounds.

Core Ingredient 01
Curcumin
Standardized Turmeric Extract · Phytosome Complex
🌿
🌿 Curcuma longa rhizome extract

The primary polyphenol from turmeric, curcumin has been extensively studied for its role in modulating the NF-κB inflammatory signaling pathway. Its lipophilic nature enables blood-brain barrier penetration, making it a subject of significant interest in neurological research. NervLock Nerve Care uses a phospholipid complex (phytosome) form to substantially enhance bioavailability.

1
Modulates NF-κB signaling to support balanced neural inflammatory environment
2
Phytosome technology delivers significantly enhanced bioavailability vs. standard curcumin
3
Lipophilic structure allows blood-brain barrier penetration — unique among polyphenols
Core Ingredient 02
Aflapin®
Patented Boswellia serrata Extract · High-AKBA
🌳
® Laila Nutraceuticals Patented Extract

Aflapin® is a next-generation Boswellia serrata extract standardized for high concentrations of AKBA (acetyl-11-keto-β-boswellic acid). Unlike conventional Boswellia extracts, Aflapin® selectively targets the 5-LOX enzyme pathway — a key mediator of leukotriene biosynthesis — supporting a balanced inflammatory microenvironment in neural tissues.

1
High-AKBA standardization ensures consistent potency and superior bioactivity
2
Selective 5-LOX inhibition complements curcumin's NF-κB action
3
Dual-pathway synergy with curcumin for comprehensive neural support
