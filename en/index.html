WHY BRAIN HEALTH

Why Brain Health Matters

The brain supports memory, attention, movement, emotion, sleep, and the ability to navigate daily life.

Brain health is shaped over time by lifestyle choices, medical history, and environmental factors — many of which can be addressed before symptoms appear.

Yet early changes are often subtle and easy to dismiss.

NervLock exists to make evidence-based brain health information accessible, honest, and practical — without replacing the role of medical evaluation.

Research suggests that approximately 45% of dementia risk factors may be modifiable.

Source: Lancet Commission on Dementia Prevention, 2024

In some neurodegenerative conditions, pathological changes may begin years or decades before noticeable symptoms appear.

Source: NIA-AA Research Framework, 2018

Brain health is meaningfully influenced by sleep quality, nutrition, physical activity, and stress management — all modifiable aspects of daily life.
